SSKVK, SINGJAMEI

SRI SRI KAUSHAL VIKAS KENDRA, SINGJAMEI

SRI SRI KAUSHAL VIKAS KENDRA, SINGJAMEI, a computer training centre under the aegis of PRIME MINISTER KAUSHAL VIKAS YOJNA (PMKVY). SSKVK, SINGJAMEI is an exclusive institute to make the youth of the nation, computer literate in partnership with SRI SRI RURAL DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM TRUST. It was inaugurated by SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL, member of parliament from DAMOH LOK SABHA constituency on 10th Dec, 2016.

The objective of our Institute is to encourage skill development for youth by providing Certificates and monetary assistance to the eligible candidates through various Government projects like Mudra Loans and others. We at SSKVK, enable and mobilize a large number of Manipuri Youth to take up skill training and become employable and earn their livelihood. Increase productivity of the existing workforce and align the training and certification to the needs of the state and country at large.

Currently, only a very small portion of Manipur's workforce has any formal skill training. Not surprisingly, therefore, several youths of our state face low income. We at SSKVK, train them and certify them through the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojna (PMKVY) which is the flagship outcome-based skill training scheme of the new Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (MSDE). This skill certification and reward scheme aim to enable and mobilize a large number of Manipuri Youth to take up skill training and become employable and earn their livelihood.
